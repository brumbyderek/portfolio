Experience

Currently, I serve in a dual role as both the Lead UI / UX Designer of Soul Refiner and as the Lead Graphic Designer for KingdomWorks Studios.

Soul Refiner is a digital streaming platform that brings cinematic small group courses to churches and provides in-depth digital tools for both personal study and group accountability.

KingdomWorks Studios creates the content for Soul Refiner as well as produces feature-length, faith-based films. I design all of the marketing materials to promote these films, such as emails, websites, and posters.

In addition to providing design leadership at these two companies, I have worked with many companies to refine their brand imaging. I have provided direction in the following areas: UI/UX, XD, Branding, Packaging, and Marketing Design.
