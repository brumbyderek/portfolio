A KingdomWorks Studios Company

Soul Refiner is a SaaS video streaming and small group platform for churches and their members. The Soul Refiner platform features interactive tools for managing these groups.

OBJECTIVE

I was hired by KingdomWorks Studios to create a custom platform that both featured content from their existing DVD lessons and print workbooks, as well as new courses. This platform would be used by both groups and individuals, for in-person and virtual learning.

Role
Principal Product Designer
Tools

Adobe Illustrator

Timeline
February 2019 - Present

Cinematic Video Courses

I designed the watch page to show the content previews in an impactful, cinematic way. By using a simple page layout and strong imagery, viewers can easily navigate to an episode, play the video, and access the study guide for that episode.

Powerful Tools

Soul Refiner is changing the way churches do small groups digitally. I designed and coded the group management tools to appear technological and futuristic to reflect this innovation. I created custom dynamic graphs that respond to a user's data, giving members and leaders clear, impactful insights into both personal and group growth.

Series Artwork

To differentiate each series, I created unique imagery for the browse page, and each series page. I custom 3D modeled each of these illustrative scenes to relate to the subject matter of each series and have a dramatic, cinematic aesthetic throughout to establish the visual tone for Soul Refiner's content.

Interactive Study Guide

I designed the study guides with rich features to enhance a user's learning. Given the ability to listen as an audiobook, highlight text, and view inline excerpts, users are better able to relate to the lessons and retain the information. I also created questionaires that can be shared with other group members, to provide opportunities for enhanced collaboration and long-term accountability.
