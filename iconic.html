4-Pack Package Design

Iconic partnered with me to design packaging for their line of 4-pack protein drinks. I delivered final print artwork for the bottles and the 4-pack box, as well as 3D product renderings for use in ecommerce stores and in promotional materials.

Protein Powder Package Design

Iconic partnered with me to design packages for their line of protein powders. I created custom illustrations for the packaging back, provided the final print artwork, and created 3d product renderings for ecommerce stores and in promotional materials.

Experiential Design

I designed a number of touchpoints for Iconic Protein's event and tradeshow presence, including booth and tent design, apparel, banners, and custom hand-lettering used in custom wooden and neon signs.
