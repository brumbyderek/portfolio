Simple Mills is a natural food brand making delicious gluten-free snacks and baking mixes with real-food ingredients and nothing artificial, ever.

OBJECTIVE

Simple Mills partnered with me to create their marketing campaigns including large-format print ads, as well as digital publications, ecommerce storefront design, and point of purchase experiences. I helped reinvision the brand style in marketing through a prominent use of brand color and lifestyle photography with minimalist type.

Amazon Storefront Design

I designed the layout of the simple Mills Amazon storefront. The page is minimal in content and helps shoppers to easily navigate through the various product lines.

Out-of-Home Ad Campaign

I designed an Out-of-Home campaign that comprehensively covered multiple main train stations in downtown Chicago as well as bus shelters and full bus wraps. I executed all design deliverables, as well as photography and retouching for this campaign.

Seasonal Brand Magazine

I partnered with Simple Mills to create their seasonal magazines for fall and winter 2018, containing recipes and articles about nutrition and wellness. I did all the image editing and graphic design for the magazine.

Shipper Displays

Simple Mills partnered with me to create shippers for their various product lines. I designed the header card and art directed the food photoshoot.
